# Code of Conduct

TAIS is an open research project. Contributors are expected to interact respectfully and constructively.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful.
- Assume good intent.
- Welcome newcomers.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Report negative scientific results honestly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Deliberate disruption.
- Misrepresentation of results.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participants who violate these expectations.
